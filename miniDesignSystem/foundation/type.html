Playfair Display & Playfair

Minor Third Scale / Base size: 16px

I chose Playfair for it's simple elegance and charm. It gives a feeling of luxury without feeling unapproachable. It's display and general variations made it easy to pair with itself. However, as a display type I wanted something a little heavier and set it to 700 by default.
