Nia

Design System

Nia [nee • ah] - (Swahili) Purpose.


This design system was created for my professional UX and Software Engineering porfolio. It's aesthetic was inspired by my middle name Niara which means "Woman with a purpose", derived from it's root Swahili word Nia.
Splitting this definition in to two parts - Woman and Purpose - this system embodies Sophistication, Passion, Creativity, and Modernity with a bit of Nerdy Fun.

It embodies me.
